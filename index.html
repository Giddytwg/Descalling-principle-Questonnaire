Descaling Principles Self-Assessment

A test Build for Robert Briese's Upwork Job Post by Tope Gideon

Rate how closely your organization aligns with each descaling principle. 1 = Not at all · 5 = Fully adopted

1. Specialist Roles → Teams

Are responsibilities like release, QA, and architecture handled by teams rather than individual specialists?

2. Resource Thinking → People Thinking

Do team members work strictly within role boundaries or are they encouraged to grow and contribute across disciplines?

3. Organizing Around Tech → Customer Value

Is your org structure tech-centric or customer-centric?

4. Independent Teams → Cross-Team Cooperation

How well do teams collaborate on shared goals versus working in silos?

5. Coordinate to Integrate → Continuous Integration

How often do teams integrate their work into a shared codebase?

6. Projects → Products

Is work managed as fixed-scope projects or ongoing product development?

7. Many Small Products → Few Broad Products

How unified are your product backlogs and ownership structures?

8. Technical → User-Centric Backlog Items

What percentage of backlog items could an end-user understand without extra explanation?

9. Remote → Co-Located Collaboration

How many days per week are teams co-located together physically?
